1. What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete the following data:

Data Type Deletion Timeline
Account information (email, name, password)Within 30 days
Uploaded PDF documents + extracted textWithin 30 days
Generated Q&A resultsWithin 30 days
Transaction historyRetained 90 days (accounting legal requirement), then deleted
Submitted feedbackWithin 30 days
Agent pipeline logsWithin 30 days
2. How to Request Deletion
Via Form on This Page

Enter your registered email in the form above. We send a confirmation link to that address.

After you click the link in the email, your request is recorded and processed within 30 days.

Via Manual Email

Send email to:

duy226466@student.nctu.edu.vn

Subject: Account Deletion Request
Body: your registered email address

3. Processing Steps
  1. You receive a confirmation email — click the link to complete your request.
  2. We record the confirmed request within 3 business days.
  3. Data is deleted from the main database within 30 days.
  4. A confirmation email will be sent once the process is complete.
  5. After deletion, the account cannot be recovered.
4. Data That Cannot Be Immediately Deleted

Transaction history is retained for up to 90 days after account deletion due to legal requirements for financial record retention. This data is anonymized and not linked to your account.
